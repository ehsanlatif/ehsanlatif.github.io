Ehsan Latif

AI Research Scientist

I am an AI Researcher and Engineer with over 8 years of experience advancing natural language processing, large language models (LLMs), and multi-agent/multi-robot systems, with a strong focus on safe, trustworthy, and sustainable AI.

I am skilled in training and optimizing transformer-based architectures (BERT, GPT, LLaMA, etc.), efficient inference deployment, and integrating LLMs into autonomous decision-making and coordination frameworks. I also have experienced in designing experiments, developing novel algorithms, and creating evaluation methodologies for reinforcement learning, evolutionary AI, and open-ended intelligence research. Furthemore, I am driven by a passion for AI for Good, bridging fundamental research with impactful applications that prioritize robustness, safety, and long-term societal benefit.
